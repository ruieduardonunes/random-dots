random dots

Explaining complex ideas is tricky.
This tool, inpired by Stefan Saigmister's A New Point Of View, will help you think in new ways about the problems and concepts you want to communicate.

get started

Click on the displayed word to get a random new one, or use the button bellow instead. You can also click on play with images to get random images instead.

Everytime a new one appears, try to think how that word, or image would help you describe the problem or idea you have at hand.
